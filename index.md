# MapLink Documentation

MapLink is a UE5 editor plugin paired with a Blender addon. It bridges `.blend` file hierarchies into Unreal Engine 5 for accurate location authoring at production scale.

Full nested `.blend` hierarchies are replicated in Unreal. This makes map work delegable: outsource or freelance the authoring of maps, sections of maps, or individual assets entirely in Blender, and keep your Unreal project's art and level design in sync — without ever opening the Unreal project. No project setup, no onboarding for the contributor, only `.blend` files in and out.

**UE5 plugin:** v1.9.2 · **Blender addon:** v1.4.6.3

**Tested against:** UE 5.7.1 · Blender 5.0
